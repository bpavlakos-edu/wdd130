Overview

Purpose

Our purpose is to teach the audience as much about my hobbies as possible, in a way they'll remember. We will achieve this goal through eye-catching CSS, interactive graphics (like image maps or interactive text highlighting), and ensuring that the content we teach is simple enough for beginners, and still useful to those who already have experience. We will also be providing links to the many, low cost or free, tools and tutorials, that I use each day to accomplish my goals, even ones that aren't directly related to content of the website. All this will be done, in the hope that the audience will not only learn how to do my hobbies, but also be inspired to overcome the challenges in their own.

Audience

Our target audience is anyone who is willing to learn something new, especially those who want to learn more about my technology-related hobbies, we'll try to appeal to them visually, because if they see something awesome, they'll try to figure out how it works, and learn from it. Another important target audience, are those who's hobbies are hindered by seemingly impossible challenges. The experiences and the resources I provide are here to both inspire, and directly assist them so they can overcome their challenges. Many of the free tools and tutorials I use on a daily basis, have been around for years, I've simply just never known about them before now. Other tools, are brand new, but also obscure, making them difficult to locate unless you stumble upon them by chance. I'm confident that anyone who visits my website will learn gain something, whether that's a tool, a new way of thinking, or even just the idea to look for resources they haven't utilized yet.
