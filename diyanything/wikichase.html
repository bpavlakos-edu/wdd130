Wiki-Chase Generator

What is a Wiki-Chase?

A wiki chase is a game you can play using any wiki-style website. You start by picking a wiki article to start from, then you pick an article (or topic) to reach. You race to the second article from the first, using only the hyperlinks found in the article itself, or any articles it links to. You repeat this process until you finally reach your target article. You can continue your chase by picking another article to reach, starting from where you finished. Still confused? Read the Wikipedia article on Wikiraces for ideas on how to play!

How do I win?

There are many ways to win a wiki-chase, and it all depends on what you decide winning is. Some people choose a winner based on speed alone, while others might choose the winner based on who can reach the finish with the least amount of articles in between. Choose the goal you like the most!

What is this tool for?

Picking an article requires thinking, and no one wants to think while playing a game! So use this tool to choose pages for you at random, you can even share your list with someone else! Pretty neat right?

Chasing is more fun with friends, share this list with someone and let the race begin!

Confirmed Compatible Wiki Urls:

/w/ Api Path:

  • *any*.wikipedia.org/w/api.php
  • *any*.wikibooks.org/w/api.php
  • www.mediawiki.org/w/api.php
  • wiki.blender.org/w/api.php
  • biosector01.com/w/api.php
  • bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/w/api.php

/wiki/ Api Path:

  • wiki.inkscape.org/wiki/api.php

Custom Api Path:

  • *any*.fandom.com/api.php

Wiki Urls with Errors:

CORS Header URL Mismatch:

  • wiki.audacityteam.org/w/api.php

Doesn't Use the MediaWiki API:

  • wiki.openttd.org

Any website powered by the mediawiki.org api should work, as long as it supports Special:Random, and doesn't have any errors!
